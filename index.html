About

    I am an Assistant Researcher at the State Key Laboratory of Information Security, Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. I earned a PhD degree in Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2021, where I worked with Processor Rui Hou. I received the ME degree from the School of Electronic Engineering, University Of Electronic Science and Technology of China in 2017 and the BE degree from the School of Electrical Engineering and Automation, Henan Polytechnic University in 2014. My research interest lies in the areas of computer architecture and security, with a focus on side channel attacks and defense.
