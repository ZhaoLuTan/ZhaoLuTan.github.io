About

   Dr. Rui Hou’s major research interest includes CPU architecture and security, as well as data center server design. Before joined IIE, he was a professor of Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He published over 30 papers in international conferences and journals, and got more than 50 patents. Rui has also served more than ten times on conference organizing committees in international academia community.
